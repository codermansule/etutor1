# Getting Started with ETUTOR

Welcome to **ETUTOR**, the online tutoring marketplace that combines live video classrooms, AI-powered coaching, and gamified learning to make education more effective and engaging.

## Create Your Account

Signing up takes less than a minute. Head to the registration page, enter your email, and choose a password. You can also sign in with Google for faster access. Once registered, you will land on your student dashboard where you can complete your profile and set your learning preferences.

## Find the Right Tutor

Use the tutor search to filter by subject, availability, time zone, rating, and language. Each tutor profile includes an intro video, verified reviews, and their teaching philosophy so you can make an informed choice before booking.

## Book Your First Lesson

Select an available time slot, confirm through Stripe-secured checkout, and you are all set. You will receive a confirmation email with a link to your LiveKit classroom. The room supports video, screen sharing, a collaborative whiteboard, and real-time chat.

## Level Up with AI and Gamification

After your lesson, use the AI tutor for practice quizzes and study plan suggestions. Earn XP for completing sessions, maintain streaks for daily activity, and unlock badges as you hit milestones. Check the leaderboard to see how you compare with other learners on the platform.
